About Me

Who I Am:

I'm a passionate and experienced Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) based in Brussels, Belgium. With over a decade of experience, I specialize in supporting children with autism and special needs. I hold a Master's Degree in Clinical Psychology and a Bachelor's Degree in Applied Psychology.

What I Do:

I specialize in assessing individual child skills and developing personalized programs using methodologies such as VB-MAPP, ABLLS-R, and EFL. I offer supervision, parent consultation, and training in applied behavior analysis to promote learning and reduce challenging behaviors.

My Approach:

I'm dedicated to developing essential skills in children, focusing on communication, play, and self-care abilities within a supportive environment. Traits like empathy, calmness, and creativity are integral to my approach, enabling me to positively impact lives.
